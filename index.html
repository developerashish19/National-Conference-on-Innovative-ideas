Introduction

Startups are rapidly emerging as the backbone of economic development, job creation, and technological advancement in India. In alignment with the Government of India‚Äôs Startup India Mission and various state-level entrepreneurship programs, there is a need to cultivate awareness, provide knowledge, and encourage innovation among students, entrepreneurs, and industry stakeholders.

This National Conference aims to bring together academicians, aspiring entrepreneurs, industry experts, investors, policymakers, and startup founders under one platform to share insights, success stories, challenges, and opportunities in the startup ecosystem.

Objectives

  • To create awareness about the role of startups in economic and social development.
  • To guide aspiring entrepreneurs on idea generation, business models, and fundraising opportunities.
  • To provide a platform for interaction between students, entrepreneurs, and industry experts.
  • To discuss government schemes, incubation facilities, and funding opportunities for startups.
  • To showcase innovative startup success stories and inspire new ventures.

Proposed Themes / Sessions

  • Startup Ecosystem in India and State Initiatives
  • From Idea to Execution: Building a Scalable Startup
  • Funding Opportunities: Angel Investors, Venture Capital, and Government Grants
  • Innovation, Technology, and Sustainability in Startups
  • Success Stories: Lessons from Young Entrepreneurs
  • Panel Discussion: Challenges and Opportunities for Startups in Tier-2 and Tier-3 Cities

Expected Participants

  • Students from engineering, management, and other professional courses
  • Budding entrepreneurs and startup founders
  • Academicians and researchers
  • Industry professionals and investors
  • Policy makers and incubation centre representatives

Expected Outcomes

  • Enhanced awareness and understanding of the startup ecosystem.
  • Motivation and guidance for students to take up entrepreneurship.
  • Networking opportunities between entrepreneurs, industry, and academia.
  • Identification of potential collaborations, incubation, and funding avenues.
  • Strengthening the entrepreneurial culture at the state level.

Resource Persons / Speakers

Successful startup founders, industry experts, investors, officials from Startup India, Ministry of MSME, and academicians.
